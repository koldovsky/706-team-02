About Us

PROFESSIONAL BOXING SCHOOL

Our boxing school offers training for amateurs and professionals. If you are looking for a way to defend yourself, we will teach you how to dump anyone with one kick. If you want to get in shape, boxing is the perfect combination of strength and cardio workouts. Our school has the best trainers in New York to help you become a really gifted boxer.

Effective workouts

We offer a universal program for adults and children that will help you keep fit.

Proper kicks

We will teach you how to throw kicks properly, and what are the most effective places to kick.
